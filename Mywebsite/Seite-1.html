About me

My name is Ulrich Lösener and I am from Heidelberg, Germany. I am currently a graduate student of Methodology and Statistics for the Behavioural, Biomedical and Social Sciences at Utrecht University.

These days, I am exploring how to create and evaluate formalized models in psychological science. This project is supervised by Oisín Ryan and Noémi Schuurman from the Dynamic Modeling Lab.

 Education

  • BSc Psychology at Albert-Ludwigs-University Freiburg, Germany
  • MSc Clinical Psychology at Utrecht University (Summa Cum Laude)
  • MSc Methodology and Statistics for the Behavioural, Biomedical and Social Sciences (pending)

 Interests
